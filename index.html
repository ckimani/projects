    who are we?

    We, at Land Deals, provide you with any kind of information relating to land starting from its availability to current issuesmin Kenya. A few years back, many people had to travel for long distances to look for land, not even sure if the hired middle man met your expectations. Getting a Title Deed was another hurdle especially since, there was no realible information on the processes involved. To close these and other gaps, Land Deals was born. It has been in operation for more than 7 years and the feedback we get from our beneficiaries is always positive.



    Any information on Land Deals is well researched and backed up with supporting documents, especially for the sensitive information. Land Deals holds a large database of:where pieces of lands that are on sale are located, sellers, and price among others

    Land Deal also keeps you up to date with the current trends in the land sector.
